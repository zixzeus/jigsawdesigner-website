JigsawDesigner Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 24, 2025

Introduction

JigsawDesigner respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our JigsawDesigner application.

Information We Collect

JigsawDesigner is designed with privacy in mind:

Information We DO NOT Collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • Contact information (email, phone numbers)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Network activity monitoring

Information We May Access:

  • Local Files: The app accesses files you explicitly choose to open or save
  • System Preferences: Basic system settings for app functionality
  • File System: Only for saving and opening your jigsaw design projects

How We Use Your Information

  • File Operations: To save and open your jigsaw design projects
  • App Functionality: To provide core features like drawing, editing, and exporting
  • Local Storage: All your designs are stored locally on your device

Data Storage and Security

  • All your designs and data are stored locally on your Mac
  • We do not transmit your personal data to external servers
  • No cloud storage or remote backup is performed by the app
  • Your files remain under your complete control

Third-Party Services

JigsawDesigner does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
