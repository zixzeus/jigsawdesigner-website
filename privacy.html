JigsawDesigner Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 25, 2025

Effective Date: September 25, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to JigsawDesigner! We take your privacy very seriously. This privacy policy details how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

JigsawDesigner ("we", "app", or "service") is developed and maintained by Xin Zou.

2. Information Collection

2.1 Information We Collect

JigsawDesigner is a local application focused on protecting your privacy:

  • Design Files: Your jigsaw designs and project files are stored entirely locally on your device
  • App Settings: Your preferences and configuration information are stored on your device
  • Usage Statistics: We do not collect your usage behavior data
  • Personal Information: We do not require you to provide personal identification information

2.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone, etc.)
  • Device identifiers and tracking information
  • Location information
  • Your design content and creative data
  • Usage behavior and analytics data

3. Information Use

Since we do not collect personal data:

  • All your design files and project data remain on your device
  • We cannot access your creative content
  • App functionality runs entirely locally without network connection
  • No data is transmitted to our servers

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

  • All data is stored on your device
  • Uses system-level security measures to protect your files
  • Supports iCloud sync (optional, controlled by Apple)

4.2 Security Measures

  • App uses Apple's sandbox technology to ensure security
  • Follows Apple's security best practices
  • Regular updates to fix security vulnerabilities

5. Third-Party Services

JigsawDesigner is an independent app that does not integrate third-party analytics or advertising services:

  • Does not use Google Analytics or similar services
  • Does not display advertisements
  • Does not integrate social media SDKs
  • Only uses Apple system frameworks

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages, including children under 13:

  • Does not collect any children's personal information
  • Does not require account registration
  • Runs entirely locally, safe and reliable

7. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties because:

  • We do not collect personal data
  • There is no server-side data storage
  • We do not perform data analysis or processing

8. Your Rights

As a user, you have complete control over your data:

  • Access Rights: You can access all files on your device at any time
  • Deletion Rights: You can delete the app and all related data
  • Export Rights: You can export your design files
  • Choice Rights: You can choose whether to enable iCloud sync

9. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We do not use any cookies or tracking technologies:

  • App does not include web browsing functionality
  • Does not use online analytics tools
  • Does not perform user behavior tracking

10. International Data Transfer

Since we do not collect or transmit any data:

  • No cross-border data transfer
  • Complies with privacy regulations of all countries
  • Follows GDPR and CCPA requirements

11. Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time:

  • Major changes will be notified through app updates
  • Latest version will be published on our website
  • We recommend reviewing this policy regularly

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us:

  • Email: zixzeus@gmail.com
  • Technical Support: zixzeus@gmail.com
  • Website: https://zixzeus.github.io/jigsawdesigner-website

Summary

JigsawDesigner Promise:
